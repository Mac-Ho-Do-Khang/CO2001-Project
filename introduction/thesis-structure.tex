\section{Thesis structure}
There are four chapters in this multidisciplinary project:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Chapter 1 briefly describes the background of the project, the motivation, and the objectives. The structure of the thesis is also presented in this chapter.
    \item Chapter 2 is dedicated to presenting the foundational knowledge for each subtopic.    
    \item Chapter 3 deep dives into the project’s implementation, including the design and development of the prototype, as well as the experiment setup and results.
    \item Chapter 4 summarizes the whole project and the plan for future development.
\end{itemize}
