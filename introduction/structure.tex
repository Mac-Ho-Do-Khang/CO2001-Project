\section{Report structure}

There are six chapters in this project report:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \hyperref[chapter:introduction]{Chapter 1} briefly describes the background of the project, the motivation, and the objectives. The structure of the thesis is also presented in this chapter.
    \item \hyperref[chapter:theoretical-background]{Chapter 2} is dedicated to presenting the foundational knowledge for each subtopic.
    \item \hyperref[chapter:related-work]{Chapter 3} reviews the existing literature and projects that are relevant to the research topic, highlighting their contributions and limitations.
    \item \hyperref[chapter:components]{Chapter 4} details the software, equipment, and materials used in the project, providing an overview of the tools and resources that were essential for the successful completion of the project.
    \item \hyperref[chapter:development]{Chapter 5} describes the design and development process of the project, including the implementation of the software and hardware components, as well as the testing and validation of the integrated system.
    \item \hyperref[chapter:conclusion]{Chapter 6} summarizes the key findings and contributions of the project, discusses the limitations and challenges encountered during the research, and suggests potential directions for future work in this area.
\end{itemize}
