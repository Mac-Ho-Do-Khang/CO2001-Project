\subsection{Fusion 360}

Fusion 360 is a cloud-based 3D Computer-Aided Design (CAD), Computer-Aided Manufacturing (CAM), and Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) tool for product design and manufacturing. It combines industrial and mechanical design, simulation, collaboration, and machining in a single package. The software is widely used for designing complex parts and assemblies, creating detailed drawings, and preparing models for 3D printing.

Fusion 360 offers a range of features including parametric modeling, sculpting, and direct modeling, making it suitable for both beginners and experienced designers. Therefore, in this project, it was used to design the custom housing for the ESP32 TTGO T-Display, ensuring that it met the required specifications and was optimized for 3D printing.
