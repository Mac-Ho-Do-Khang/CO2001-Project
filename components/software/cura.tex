\subsection{UltiMaker Cura}

Cura is a slicing software developed by UltiMaker, widely used in the 3D printing community. It converts 3D models into instructions (G-code) that 3D printers can understand. Cura empowers users with hundreds of customizable parameters, intelligent print optimization algorithms, and extensive material profiles. With enough knowledge and experience, one can leverage Cura's capabilities to achieve high-quality prints, optimize print times, and reduce material waste.
